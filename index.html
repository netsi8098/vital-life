Privacy Policy

How Vital Life handles information

This policy explains how the Vital Life brand — and the software application we use to publish our own short videos to social platforms such as TikTok and YouTube — handles data. In plain terms: our software exists to upload our own content to our own connected accounts. It is not a consumer product, and it does not gather information about the people who watch our videos.

1Who we are

“Vital Life” (“we”, “us”, “our”) is an independent content project that produces short, uplifting wellness tips and daily faith reflections. We operate a companion application that automates publishing of the videos we create to our own social media accounts.

2What our software does

Our application connects to a social platform’s official publishing API (for example, TikTok’s Content Posting API) using an account we own and control. Its only function is to take a video file and caption that we produced and post it to that connected account on a schedule. There is no public sign-up, no user accounts, and no viewer-facing features.

3Information we access

  • Authorization tokens for the social account we connect, granted by the platform’s official login flow, so the software can publish on that account’s behalf.
  • The videos and captions we create ourselves, which are the content being uploaded.
  • Basic publish results returned by the platform (such as a post ID or a success/error status) so we can confirm a video posted.

4What we do not collect

We do not collect, store, buy, or process personal information about viewers or any other individuals — no names, contacts, viewing history, biometric data, location, or advertising identifiers. We have no analytics on people who watch our videos beyond the aggregate counts the platforms show every creator.

5How we use the information

Authorization tokens are used only to publish our videos to the connected account and to refresh that access so posting can continue without repeated logins. Publish results are used only to confirm success and to avoid posting duplicates. We do not use any of this for advertising, profiling, or resale.

6Sharing and selling

We do not sell or rent any data. We do not share information with third parties except the social platform we are publishing to (which necessarily receives the video we upload), and we handle everything in accordance with that platform’s developer terms and policies.

7Retention and security

Access tokens are stored privately on our own controlled system and are refreshed or replaced as the platform requires; they can be revoked at any time. Our created videos and captions are retained as part of our normal content archive. We take reasonable technical measures to keep credentials confidential and limited to the publishing function.

8Children’s privacy

Vital Life content is general-audience inspiration and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9Your choices & contact

Because we do not collect personal data from viewers, there is generally nothing for an individual to request or delete. Platform account access can be revoked by us at any time through the platform’s settings. Questions about this policy can be sent to the contact address below.
